Ferrari Enzo

Description:

The Ferrari Enzo, named after the company's founder, Enzo Ferrari, is an iconic supercar that debuted in 2002. Designed as a limited-production model, only 400 units were produced. The Enzo embodies Ferrari's cutting-edge engineering and racing heritage, featuring a lightweight carbon-fiber body, advanced aerodynamics, and a mid-mounted 6.0-liter V12 engine generating around 660 horsepower. Its Formula 1-inspired technology, such as the electrohydraulic shift transmission and carbon-ceramic brakes, delivers exhilarating performance and precise handling. With a top speed of over 200 mph and a 0-60 mph time of around 3.3 seconds, the Enzo represents the pinnacle of Ferrari's road-going performance vehicles. Its rarity, engineering prowess, and timeless design make it a coveted collector's item among automotive enthusiasts.

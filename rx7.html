Han's Mazda Rx7

Description:

Han's Mazda RX-7 is a highly modified sports car featured in the "Fast and Furious" film series. Driven by the character Han Seoul-Oh, portrayed by Sung Kang, this iconic vehicle is a third-generation Mazda RX-7 FD3S. The car is known for its sleek design, distinctive styling, and high-performance modifications, making it a standout in the street racing scene depicted in the movies. Han's RX-7 is often associated with his calm and composed demeanor, reflecting the character's affinity for precision driving and calculated risk-taking.

Lamborghini Centario

Description:

The Lamborghini Centenario is a limited-edition supercar produced by the Italian manufacturer Lamborghini to commemorate the 100th birthday of its founder, Ferruccio Lamborghini. Introduced in 2016, the Centenario boasts an extraordinary design characterized by aggressive lines, aerodynamic elements, and exotic materials. Underneath its striking exterior lies a powerful naturally aspirated V12 engine, producing over 750 horsepower, capable of propelling the car from 0 to 60 mph in under 2.8 seconds and achieving a top speed of over 217 mph. The Centenario features advanced technology, including all-wheel drive and dynamic steering systems, enhancing its performance and handling prowess. With only 40 units produced (20 coupes and 20 roadsters), the Lamborghini Centenario represents a rare and exclusive offering in the world of hypercars.

$20m

Mileage: 100km

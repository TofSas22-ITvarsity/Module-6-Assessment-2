Porsche Carrera GT

Description:

The Porsche Carrera GT is an iconic supercar that was produced by Porsche between 2004 and 2007. It boasts a mid-engine layout and a naturally aspirated 5.7-liter V10 engine, producing around 612 horsepower, paired with a six-speed manual transmission. With its lightweight construction, advanced aerodynamics, and race-inspired design, the Carrera GT delivers exhilarating performance and handling. It accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just under 3.5 seconds and has a top speed of over 200 mph. The Carrera GT is revered for its precise steering, superb agility, and thrilling driving experience. It remains a benchmark in automotive engineering and is highly coveted by collectors and enthusiasts alike.

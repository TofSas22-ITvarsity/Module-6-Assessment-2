Mclaren P1

Description:

The McLaren P1 is a hypercar that embodies the pinnacle of automotive engineering and performance. Launched in 2013, it's part of the exclusive 'holy trinity' of hybrid hypercars along with the LaFerrari and the Porsche 918 Spyder. With a sleek, aerodynamic design and a hybrid powertrain consisting of a twin-turbocharged V8 engine combined with an electric motor, the P1 delivers mind-blowing acceleration and handling. Its advanced technology includes features like adjustable suspension, active aerodynamics, and a hybrid powertrain that offers both blistering performance and impressive fuel efficiency. Limited to just 375 units, the McLaren P1 represents the epitome of automotive innovation and exclusivity.

Price:

$10m

Mileage: 180km
